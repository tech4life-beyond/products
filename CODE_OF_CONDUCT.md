# Code of Conduct

Tech4Life & Beyond is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and mission-aligned environment.

## Our standards
We expect:
- Respectful collaboration
- Honest, evidence-based discussion
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Mission-first behavior (human/animal/planet benefit)

## Enforcement
Repository maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that harms the community or the mission.
