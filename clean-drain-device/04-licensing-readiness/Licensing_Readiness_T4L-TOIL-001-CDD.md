## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the conditions under which the **Clean Drain Device** may be evaluated, licensed, manufactured, and commercialized by third parties under the **TOIL framework**.

Its purpose is to:
- Prevent misuse or unauthorized commercialization
- Clarify manufacturer responsibilities
- Protect creators, users, and the Tech4Life ecosystem
- Enable scalable, ethical licensing without friction

---

## 2. Licensing Eligibility

The Clean Drain Device may be licensed to:
- HVAC manufacturers
- HVAC accessory manufacturers
- OEM partners and distributors
- Retail brands (through approved manufacturing partners)

Licensing is **not automatic**. Any commercial use requires a **separate TOIL Royalty Agreement** executed with Tech4Life & Beyond LLC.

---

## 3. Permitted Commercial Activities (With License)

Once a Royalty Agreement is executed, a Licensee may:
- Manufacture the Clean Drain Device at scale
- Distribute and sell the product commercially
- Integrate the device into HVAC systems or kits
- Adapt materials or manufacturing methods for regulatory compliance
- Market the product under agreed branding conditions

All permitted uses must:
- Maintain TOIL attribution
- Respect ethical and safety constraints
- Comply with all applicable laws and standards

## 4. Prohibited Activities (Strict)

The following actions are strictly prohibited without written authorization:
- Manufacturing or selling without a signed Royalty Agreement
- Filing patents or exclusive IP claims derived from this design
- Removing or altering Tech4Life & Beyond attribution or TOIL notice
- Misrepresenting authorship or origin
- Modifying the product to bypass safety or maintenance benefits
- Using the product in deceptive, exploitative, or unsafe applications

Any violation may result in:
- Immediate license revocation
- Legal enforcement under license breach
- Public record of non-compliance if required

Licensed manufacturers are solely responsible for:
