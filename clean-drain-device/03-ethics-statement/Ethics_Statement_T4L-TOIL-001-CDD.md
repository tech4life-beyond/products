**Product ID:** T4L-TOIL-001-CDD
**Legacy IDs:** T4L-2025-001
**Product Name:** Clean Drain Device
**Company:** Tech4Life & Beyond LLC

---

## 1. Purpose of This Statement

This document defines the **ethical boundaries, responsibilities, and risk considerations** associated with the Clean Drain Device.

Ethics within Tech4Life & Beyond are treated as a **design constraint**, not an afterthought. This statement is binding for all contributors, licensees, and commercial partners.

---

## 2. Core Ethical Intent

The Clean Drain Device is designed with a **preventative and protective purpose**:

- Reduce property damage caused by condensate overflow
- Prevent health risks related to mold and water exposure
- Lower unnecessary emergency service interventions

The product does **not** create dependency, addiction, or exploit failure conditions for profit.

---

## 3. Non-Harm Declaration

The Clean Drain Device:

- Does not intentionally harm humans, animals, or ecosystems
- Does not emit hazardous substances during normal operation
- Does not interfere with HVAC safety mechanisms when properly installed
- Does not introduce new failure modes beyond standard HVAC components

The device operates passively by default and activates only when optional sensing components are enabled.

---

## 4. Misuse & Risk Considerations

### Identified Risks

Potential risks include:

- Improper installation
- Incorrect electrical integration of optional sensors
- Use in incompatible HVAC systems

### Risk Mitigation

- Clear installation and compatibility documentation
- Optional components clearly labeled and isolated
- Recommendation for professional installation where required

No foreseeable misuse results in direct harm when standard HVAC practices are followed.

---

## 5. Environmental Responsibility

The Clean Drain Device is designed to:

- Support long service life
- Reduce waste by preventing equipment damage and premature replacement
- Be compatible with recyclable plastics and sustainable materials where applicable

Environmental benefit is achieved through **prevention**, not increased consumption.

---

## 6. Data & Privacy

The Clean Drain Device:

- Does not collect personal data
- Does not transmit user information
- Does not include network connectivity by default

Any external monitoring or smart integration is outside the scope of the base product and must comply with applicable data protection regulations.

## 9. Review & Evolution

This Ethics & Responsibility Statement:

Any updates must preserve the original ethical intent and preventative purpose of the product.
