## 1. Purpose

This document defines the royalty structure and distribution logic for the Clean Drain Device under the Tech4Life Open Impact License (TOIL) v1.0.

This document establishes the internal royalty allocation model. Final commercial percentages are determined through individual Royalty Agreements with licensed manufacturers or distributors.

---

## 2. Commercial Licensing Requirement

Any entity wishing to manufacture, distribute, or commercially exploit the Clean Drain Device must enter into a signed TOIL Royalty Agreement with Tech4Life & Beyond LLC.

Commercial use without agreement is prohibited.

---

## 3. Royalty Calculation Model

Unless otherwise negotiated, the default structure is:

**Royalty Base:** Net Sales Revenue
(Net sales = Gross revenue minus returns, refunds, and approved distribution costs)

**Royalty Range:** 3%–8% of Net Sales

Final percentage is negotiated based on:

- Manufacturing scale
- Territory
- Distribution channel
- Tooling investment requirements
- Exclusivity terms (if any)

## 4. Internal Royalty Allocation Framework (Redacted — Public)

Tech4Life & Beyond LLC maintains an internal royalty allocation framework to distribute proceeds to contributors and to sustain operations.

**Public note:** Specific internal allocation percentages, contributor splits, and distribution mechanics are **Confidential** and are maintained in private internal repositories. They are finalized per product and may vary based on contribution records and collaboration agreements.

For external licensing, the only binding commercial terms are those defined in the signed TOIL Royalty Agreement with the licensee.

## 5. Payment Schedule

Royalty payments from licensees shall be:

- Reported quarterly
- Paid within 30 days of quarter end
- Accompanied by detailed sales statement

Internal distribution to contributors shall occur within 15 days of receipt.

---

## 6. Transparency Requirements

Tech4Life commits to:

- Maintaining royalty ledger records
- Allowing audit rights as defined in Royalty Agreements
- Providing contributors with periodic statements

## 7. Audit Rights

Tech4Life retains the right to audit licensee sales records under terms defined in the TOIL Royalty Agreement.

## 8. International Payments

Payments may be distributed via:

- Bank transfer (ACH / Wire)
- Wise or equivalent international platform
- Digital asset settlement (if legally compliant)

All contributors must comply with tax regulations in their jurisdiction.
