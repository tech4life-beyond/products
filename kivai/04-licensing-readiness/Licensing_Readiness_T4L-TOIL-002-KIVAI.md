## 1. Purpose

This document defines Kivai’s licensing posture and readiness as a TOIL-governed platform for commercial integration.

Kivai is intended to be adopted by device manufacturers, integrators, and software providers who need a standardized execution contract between AI and physical devices.

---

## 2. Commercial Use Requirement

Commercial use requires a signed TOIL Royalty Agreement with Tech4Life & Beyond LLC.

Commercial use includes:

- Embedding Kivai in consumer or industrial devices
- Shipping products that depend on Kivai protocol/schema as a core feature
- Bundling Kivai SDK components in paid software
- Selling services that provide Kivai-based device execution

---

## 3. Licensing Value Proposition

Kivai provides commercial partners:

- Standardization across brands and ecosystems
- Safer intent execution via validation and schema compliance
- Interoperability patterns (context-sharing, routing)
- Faster integration across device lines

---

## 4. Deployment Profiles

Kivai may be licensed for:

- Consumer smart home devices
- Industrial devices and robotics
- Healthcare-adjacent devices (with additional safety requirements)
- Enterprise automation environments

Sensitive verticals may require stricter agreements and compliance addenda.

---

## 5. Readiness Status

Current readiness:

- Reference implementation and tooling exist
- Schema/API definitions are under versioned development
- Demonstration tooling exists via mock devices and tests

Kivai is **licensing-ready for pilot integrations** under controlled agreements.
Full industrial-scale licensing readiness will be declared at v1.0 stable scope completion.
