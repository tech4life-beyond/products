## 1. Purpose

This document registers Kivai as an official Tech4Life & Beyond platform asset governed under the Tech4Life Open Impact License (TOIL) v1.0.

Kivai is designated as a foundational interoperability platform to standardize intent execution between AI systems and physical device ecosystems.

---

## 2. Platform Definition

Kivai provides:

- A standardized intent schema for structured commands
- A device-facing Intent API for deterministic execution
- Context-sharing conventions for multi-device routing
- A foundation for trust, authorization, and safety policies
- Reference tooling (validator, SDK utilities, tests, mock devices)

Kivai is not an AI assistant.
Kivai is the execution and interoperability contract layer.

---

## 3. Scope of Application

Kivai is intended for:

- Tech4Life products containing electronics/software
- Third-party device manufacturers
- AI/NLP developers integrating device execution
- Smart environments requiring cross-brand interoperability
